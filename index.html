Product Description

Our thermostat comes with built-in Amazon Alexa Voice Service for easy hand-free use. Change settings with a word. It lets you view the current indoor and outdoor temperature and check the daily and weekly forecast, whether you’re away or at home. Make your heat/cool setpoints and then increase or decrease the difference between them whenever you want. Our sensor knows when you’re close by and activates the thermostat automatically to make your life easier. The recognition camera checks who you are and only allows admin users to change settings, so it’s safe and child proof. ThermoSecure keeps your house in working order in the winter so there’s no frost buildup on your windows either.
